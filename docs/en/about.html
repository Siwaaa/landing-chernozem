About us

My name is Aleksey. I was born in 1989 in Leningrad, Soviet Union, and I am a self taught video producer with more than 20 years of professional work experience under my belt.

As a producer I have worked for several state and independent TV channels in Russia (2nd Kanal, 5th Kanal in St. Petersburg, Dozhd), as well as the leading media studio Shandesign, where I did production, colour grading and editing for national and international media partners.

In 2015 I launched my own studio, Chernosyem. I do video postproduction, 3D animations, graphics and colour gradings for russian and international clients. I take pride in delivering creative and innovative solutions, that let my clients stand out, no matter how tight the deadline.
